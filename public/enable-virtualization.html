Enable Hardware Virtualization

A one-time BIOS change required for Docker. Select your computer brand below.

Dell Computers

BIOS Key: F2
  1. Restart your computer.
  2. Press F2 repeatedly as soon as the Dell logo appears.
  3. Navigate to Virtualization Support (or Advanced > Virtualization).
  4. Set Virtualization to Enabled.
  5. Press F10 to Save and Exit.
On newer Dell models, the setting may be under Advanced > Virtualization Support > Intel Virtualization Technology.

HP Computers

BIOS Key: F10
  1. Restart your computer.
  2. Press F10 repeatedly as soon as the HP logo appears.
  3. Navigate to Configuration (or Security > System Security).
  4. Find Virtualization Technology (VTx) and set to Enabled.
  5. Press F10 to Save and Exit.
On some HP laptops, try pressing Esc first, then F10 for BIOS Setup.

Lenovo Computers

BIOS Key: F1 (ThinkPad) or F2 (IdeaPad)
  1. Restart your computer.
  2. Press F1 (ThinkPad/ThinkCentre) or F2 (IdeaPad/Legion) repeatedly at the Lenovo logo.
  3. Navigate to Security > Virtualization.
  4. Set Intel (R) Virtualization Technology to Enabled.
  5. Press F10 to Save and Exit.
On Lenovo Yoga/IdeaPad, you may need to press Fn + F2 if function keys are set to media mode.

ASUS Computers

BIOS Key: DEL or F2
  1. Restart your computer.
  2. Press DEL (desktops) or F2 (laptops) repeatedly at the ASUS logo.
  3. Press F7 for Advanced Mode if needed.
  4. Navigate to Advanced > CPU Configuration.
  5. Set Intel Virtualization Technology (or SVM Mode for AMD) to Enabled.
  6. Press F10 to Save and Exit.

Acer Computers

BIOS Key: F2 or DEL
  1. Restart your computer.
  2. Press F2 (laptops) or DEL (desktops) repeatedly at the Acer logo.
  3. Navigate to Advanced tab.
  4. Find Intel Virtualization Technology and set to Enabled.
  5. Press F10 to Save and Exit.

Microsoft Surface

Volume Up + Power button
  1. Shut down your Surface completely.
  2. Press and hold the Volume Up button.
  3. While holding Volume Up, press and release the Power button.
  4. Release Volume Up when the UEFI screen appears.
  5. Navigate to Devices or Advanced.
  6. Enable Virtualization and click Exit > Restart.
Surface uses a UEFI interface instead of traditional BIOS. It looks different but works the same way.

Samsung Computers

BIOS Key: F2
  1. Restart your computer.
  2. Press F2 repeatedly at the Samsung logo.
  3. Navigate to Advanced tab.
  4. Find Intel Virtualization Technology and set to Enabled.
  5. Press F10 to Save and Exit.

LG Computers

BIOS Key: F2
  1. Restart your computer.
  2. Press F2 repeatedly at the LG logo.
  3. Navigate to Advanced tab.
  4. Find Intel Virtualization Technology and set to Enabled.
  5. Press F10 to Save and Exit.

Huawei MateBook

BIOS Key: F2
  1. Restart your computer.
  2. Press F2 repeatedly at the Huawei logo.
  3. Navigate to Advanced tab.
  4. Find Intel Virtualization Technology and set to Enabled.
  5. Press F10 to Save and Exit.

MSI Computers

BIOS Key: DEL
  1. Restart your computer.
  2. Press DEL repeatedly at the MSI logo.
  3. Navigate to OC (or Advanced) > CPU Features.
  4. Set SVM Mode (AMD) or Intel VT to Enabled.
  5. Press F10 to Save and Exit.

Not sure which key to press?

Most computers briefly show "Press [key] for Setup" during boot. Common keys are F2, DEL, F10, or Esc.

The setting is usually called Intel Virtualization Technology (Intel CPUs) or SVM Mode (AMD CPUs), found under Advanced, Security, or CPU Configuration menus.
